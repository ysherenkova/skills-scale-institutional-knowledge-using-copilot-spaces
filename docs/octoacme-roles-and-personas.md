# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates release planning and execution; ensures all release requirements are met. Communicates schedule and status to stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and execute release schedules in coordination with PM and QA leads
- Ensure all release requirements (feature completeness, sign-off, deployment readiness) are met
- Communicate release status and timelines to stakeholders
- Manage release risk and contingency planning

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, on-schedule releases
- Reduce release incidents and post-release issues
- Ensure clear go/no-go decisions with stakeholder alignment

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reviews with PM and QA
- Deployment status updates to stakeholders
- Post-release retrospective input

### Interactions
Collaborates with PM for timelines, with QA for acceptance, and with Product Leads for deployment priorities.

---

## QA Automation Lead

### Role Summary
Designs and maintains automated test suites; tracks quality metrics; communicates testing needs and results.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain automated test frameworks and suites
- Define and track quality metrics (coverage, defect rates, test pass rates)
- Communicate testing scope, progress, and results to the team
- Identify gaps in test coverage and drive improvements

### Goals
- Increase confidence in releases through automation
- Reduce manual testing overhead over time
- Provide actionable quality data to the team

### Typical Communication
- Test result reports and quality dashboards
- Sprint planning discussions on test scope
- Integration feedback to developers

### Interactions
Works with Developers for test integration, PM for test scope, and Product Manager for feature readiness.

---

## Stakeholder Engagement Coordinator

### Role Summary
Plans and executes stakeholder communications (updates, feedback sessions); ensures stakeholders are informed and involved throughout the project.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain stakeholder communication plans
- Organize and facilitate feedback sessions and stakeholder updates
- Capture and track stakeholder feedback for action
- Ensure stakeholders receive timely, accurate project information

### Goals
- Maintain stakeholder trust and engagement
- Surface stakeholder concerns early to avoid late-stage surprises
- Foster collaborative decision-making

### Typical Communication
- Regular stakeholder newsletters or briefings
- Feedback session facilitation and notes
- Escalation routing when needed

### Interactions
Interfaces with PM, Product Manager, and Sponsor to coordinate communication plans and capture feedback.

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Documents processes, release notes, and user-facing documentation; ensures clarity and usability.

### Responsibilities
- Author and maintain process documents, release notes, and user guides
- Collaborate with developers and PMs to capture changes accurately
- Review documentation for clarity, accuracy, and consistency
- Manage documentation versioning and publication

### Goals
- Ensure all audiences have clear, accessible documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service documentation
- Keep documentation in sync with product changes

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with developers and PM
- Release note drafts for stakeholder approval
- Feedback incorporation from users and reviewers

### Interactions
Collaborates with PM, Product Manager, and Developers to understand changes and produce documentation.

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Manages post-release support and incident response; tracks incoming tickets/issues; provides feedback to development teams.

### Responsibilities
- Oversee post-release support operations and incident response
- Triage and prioritize incoming support tickets and issues
- Communicate incident status and resolution timelines to affected parties
- Provide structured feedback to development teams based on support trends

### Goals
- Minimize customer impact from post-release issues
- Reduce time-to-resolution for critical incidents
- Feed support insights back into the product improvement cycle

### Typical Communication
- Incident reports and resolution updates
- Weekly support summary for PM and development leads
- Escalation paths for critical issues

### Interactions
Coordinates with Developers for bug fixes and with PM for incident communications.
